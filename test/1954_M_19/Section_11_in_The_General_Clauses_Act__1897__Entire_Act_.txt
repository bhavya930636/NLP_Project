Section 11 in The General Clauses Act, 1897
11. Measurement of distances
.In the measurement of any distance, for the purpose of any [Central Act] [Substituted by A.O.1937, for " Act of the Governor General-in-Council" .] or Regulation made after the commencement of this Act, that distance shall, unless a different intention appears, be measured in a straight line on a horizontal plane.
