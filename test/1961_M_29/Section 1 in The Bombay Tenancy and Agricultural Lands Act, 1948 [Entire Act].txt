State of Gujarat - Section
Section 1 in The Bombay Tenancy and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948
1. Short title and extent.
(1)
This Act may be called the Bombay and Agricultural Lands Act, 1948.
(2)
It extends to the [Bombay area of the State of Gujarat.] [These words were substituted for the words 'pre-Re-organisation State of Bombay excluding the transferred territories' by the Gujarat Adaptation of Laws (State and concurrent Subjects) (Third Amendment) Order, 1960.]
